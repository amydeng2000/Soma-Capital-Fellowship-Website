FAQ



Who is Startup Shadowing @Soma for?

  • Any undergrad/graduate student or new grad who aspires to build their own venture!
  • Some participating companies are looking for MBA's and students interested in biotech
  • Outstanding high school students are welcomed as well!

What can I get out of Startup Shadowing @Soma?

  • A paid, immersive, personalized experience working with founders in building a company from 0 to 1
  • Exclusive access to Soma's workshops, network, and mentorship throughout your internship and as you launch your own startup and beyond
  • A lifelong relationship with entrepreneurial peers nationwide who are together creating the future

What resources can I tap into?

  • Soma Capital's advisors, including co-founder of Scribd
  • Soma Capital's sister fund, Bow Capital, and their advisors and portfolio companies, including founder of Qualcomm and Shaq O'Neal
  • Soma Capital's LP base: founder of Tinder, government leaders globally, family offices of FAANG founders, etc

Can I choose to work at a specific company?

  • Finalists can choose up to 5 preferred companies, and your preferences will be take into consideration during companies' vetting process

Can I interact with portfolio companies other than the one I work at?

  • Yes! We will organize virtual panels and fireside chats with other seed and later stage companies in our portfolio and network

What does the incubation program look like?

  • Weekly office hours with the Soma team
  • Masterclass and fireside chats with expert founders and investors
  • Weekly or biweekly fellowship events and check-ins
  • Open channels for content sharing and discussions

How long is the fellowship? What does summer/fall cohort mean?

  • If you are in the summer cohort, you will shadow a founder over the summer, participate in the incubation program & build your own startup in the fall, and present your final pitch to Soma Capital's investment committee in late 2020
  • If you are in the fall cohort, you will shadow in the fall, build your startup over winter, and pitch at the end of Q1 2021

Will this be remote? Will this be full time?

  • Both cohorts are fully remote!
  • Startup Shadowing @Soma has two modules: internship and incubation. The internship program will be full time; the incubation program will be part time but is expected to be time intensive as working on a startup is!

What does the application timeline look like?

  • Submit the application form by June 5th, 11:59 pm PST
  • Selected semifinalists will participate in a video interview
  • Finalists for both the summer and fall cohort will be announced around June 15th
  • Companies will conduct separate interviews and make the summer cohort hiring decisions around June 22nd; fall cohort hiring decisions will be made by late July
